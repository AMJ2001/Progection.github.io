ProGection Technical Club

Ch. Brahm Prakash Government Engineering College

Bridging gap between theory and practice

ProGection Technical Clubs are College based community groups for students interested in technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a ProGection Technical Club, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
